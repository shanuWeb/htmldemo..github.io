about usWho We Are

McGee is a family business, founded in
1959 by Tom McGee.

Since then we have grown to become a respected name in the construction market specialising in decontamination, asbestos removal, demolition, civil engineering, construction and recycling services, either as standalone disciplines or as an integrated solution.
